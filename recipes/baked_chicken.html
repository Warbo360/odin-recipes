Odin's Easy Baked Chicken

Description

Whether short on time or meal prepping this chicken breasts recipe is a must have for any on the go life-style that still wants a good home cooked meal. Offering easy/short prep and cook time, and very little dishwashing after, while being able to be paired with just about any sides you could ask for this is a must have recipe for even the laziest of homecooks.

Ingredients

  • 2 chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of Kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon of freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon of ground tyme
  • 1 tablespoon of smoked paprika

Steps

  1. Let chicken thaw to room temp
  2. Pre-heat oven to 425 Farenheit
  3. Pat chicken dry with papertowl and place on baking sheet
  4. Coat chicken in olive oil and then the dry spice rub mix, I find wearing nitrile gloves makes this task easy
  5. Once oven is up to temp place the tray with the chicken in the oven, this will cook for ~18-20 minutes
  6. At the 9-10 minute mark flip the breasts and continue cooking
  7. At the 18 minute mark check the internal temp of the chicken, needs to reach 165 as carry over cooking will take it to ~170, once reached take out of the oven and allow to rest for 5-10 minutes
  8. Once rested slice the chicken across its length and enjoy with any and all sides!
